# Online Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail transaction data to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and geographic revenue distribution.

The goal of this project is to transform raw retail transaction records into actionable business insights that can support inventory planning, sales strategy, customer retention, and revenue growth initiatives.

I used Power Query to clean and transform the data, DAX to create business KPIs, SQL to validate and analyze sales metrics, and Power BI to build the final dashboard.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transaction data every day. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to understand customer behavior, product demand, and revenue performance.

This project answers questions such as:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which countries contribute the most sales?
* How does revenue change over time?
* What purchasing patterns can be identified from customer transactions?

The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer activity, product profitability, and geographic revenue trends.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains online retail transactions from a UK-based online retailer between 2010 and 2011.

Important fields include:

* Invoice Number
* Product Description
* Quantity Sold
* Invoice Date
* Unit Price
* Customer ID
* Country

After data preparation, the analysis was performed on more than 500,000 transaction records.

## Data Preparation Steps

The dataset was cleaned and prepared before dashboard development.

Main preparation steps included:

* Removed records with missing product descriptions.
* Removed transactions with invalid quantity values.
* Removed cancelled orders.
* Verified unit price values.
* Converted date columns into proper date formats.
* Created revenue calculations using quantity and unit price.
* Validated KPI calculations before visualization.

## Key Insights

* Total revenue exceeded $10.67 million.
* The United Kingdom generated the highest revenue among all countries.
* Revenue increased significantly during October through December.
* Holiday season demand contributed heavily to annual revenue performance.
* A small group of products generated a significant share of total sales.
* Customer purchasing activity increased during peak shopping periods.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory levels before peak seasonal periods.
* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products.
* Expand successful products into international markets.
* Develop customer retention campaigns targeting repeat buyers.
* Use historical revenue trends to improve demand forecasting.
